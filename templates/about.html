National Healthcare Providers

At National Healthcare Providers, we strive to treat as many people
as we can. Diabetes can be devastating, and if we can catch it soon,
our patients will have the best possible treatment. This tool is
designed to identify those who are at risk of being diabetic (1 | Yes), and it
serves to give a bit of peace to those who might have concern but
don't actually need additional lab testing (0 | No).
